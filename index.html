Project 2

The prototype of plant harmony-Happy Plant Family

The project involves four garden pots connected to an Arduino system, representing a family unit (Mom, Dad, Child, Pet). Each pot is equipped with a soil moisture sensor, display, and speaker. When one plant is thirsty, it "complains" with a sad emoji and sound. The other plants react sympathetically or cheer it on until it’s watered, fostering a unique interaction to raise awareness of plant care.
